Earn Your Wings |  Friends of The Butterfly Trees

The Butterfly Trees is a fiscally sponsored project of the Louisville Film Society. You can make a tax-deductible donation to this project by visiting this page. Upon contribution, you will receive a letter acknowledging your charitable donation to the Louisville Film Society on behalf of the project.

If you prefer to not to donate online, you may download this pdf donor form with either your credit card information or check made out to Louisville Film Society with The Butterfly Trees in the memo section and mail to LFS c/o K. Milam, 149 N. Bayly Ave, Louisville, KY 40206.



Thank you kindly for your contribution. All amounts are deeply meaningful & incredibly helpful. Individuals who contribute $250 or more will be listed in the end credits of the film if so desired.
